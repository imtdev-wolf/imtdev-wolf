### 🪨 地 | Earth — Foundation
*Chi — The Ground*

Every solution begins with understanding the terrain. I start by deeply analyzing the problem space, studying the landscape, and building on solid ground. No shortcuts. No assumptions. Just thorough groundwork before the first line of code.

> *"To know ten thousand things, know one well"*

---

### 💧 水 | Water — Adaptation  
*Sui — The Flow*

Water has no shape of its own, yet it can wear down mountains. My approach is fluid — adapting to constraints, flowing around obstacles, finding the path of least resistance while maintaining unstoppable momentum. Technology changes; the ability to adapt endures.

> *"If you know the way broadly, you will see it in everything"*

---

### 🔥 火 | Fire — Passion
*Ka — The Flame*

Fire transforms. It consumes the unnecessary and forges the essential. I build with intensity — whether it's a game economy simulator, an AI pipeline, or a weekend experiment. Every project gets full commitment, full attention, full fire.

> *"Think lightly of yourself and deeply of the world"*

---

### 💨 風 | Wind — Knowledge
*Fū — The Wind*

Wind carries seeds, scents, songs from distant lands. I learn from everywhere — game design theory, AI research, player psychology, ancient philosophy. Knowledge isn't hoarded; it's circulated, tested, refined. Study widely, but apply precisely.

> *"Study strategy over the years and achieve the spirit of the warrior"*

---

### ☁️ 空 | Void — Simplicity
*Kū — The Emptiness*

The void isn't empty — it's potential. Before complexity, seek simplicity. Before adding features, understand what's essential. The best code is no code. The best architecture is invisible. The best product feels inevitable.

> *"The ultimate aim of martial arts is not having to use them"*

## 哲学 | Philosophy

🌸 *一発必中* — One shot, one kill

> Every project I build serves a purpose. Some solve immediate problems. Others explore "what if?" questions. A few become tools I use daily. Most teach me something I didn't know yesterday.

🌸 *守破離* — Shu-Ha-Ri

> **守** Protect — Learn the rules  
> **破** Break — Question the rules  
> **離** Transcend — Create new rules

🌸 *残心* — Zanshin

> The follow-through matters as much as the strike. I finish what I start. Documentation written. Lessons learned. Code that future-me won't hate.
